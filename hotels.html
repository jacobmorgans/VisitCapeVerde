Restaurants

Américo’s Restaurante

Want a nice night out with good food. Americos which is set in the heart of Santa Maria has some of the best food on the island. From fish to pasta and meat dishes you will not be disappointed. Americos also has some of the best wine including the countries own Fogo wine where the vines grow on the active volcanic island.View More

Ocean Cafe

Situated right on the square Ocean Cafe serves the best cocktails on the island. the Cafe also serves some of the best vegetarian and vegan meals on the island. At night, the cafe is a thriving place with allot of dancing and waiters on skates.View More

Angulo Cabo verde

Angulo Cabo Verde is a beach bar owned and run by world champion wind surfer Josh Angulo. if you’re the type of person that enjoys surfing or watching it then Angulo’s is the perfect place for a beer and a pizza on the beach.View More

Hotels

Oasis Salinas Sea - £77 night

Commanding a stunning beachfront location, the Oasis Salinas Sea is a firm top pick with guests to the pretty southern town of Santa Maria. With its shimmering pools, modern style and astounding ocean views, this hotel is ideal for those looking for a charming sea side escape with a contemporary touch. View More

Hotel Morabeza - £170 night

Situated on Sal island, one of the 10 tropical islands framing the archipelago of Cape Verde, Hotel Morabeza welcomes you to remain in an enchanting setting, along the best white sandy beaches and only a couple minutes from the town of Santa Maria. View More

Odjo d'Agua - £146 night

Set right next to the beach, the Odjo d'Agua is a grand hotel which has beautiful views across the bay. stunning rooms and a brand-new infinity pool puts the hotel in the top 10 of Cape Verde. along with its renowned restaurant serving fresh lobster, it is the perfect retreat. View More

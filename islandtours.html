Island Tours

Turtle Walks

Enjoy a 2-hour guided tour about turtles. Walk along the beaches where you get a chance to see logger head turtles during nesting. The turtle walks run from July to October so head to the contact us page now if you want to book. spaces are limited and go fast due to the nature of the tour.

Island Tours

Shark Visit

Enjoy a day out with professional tour guides learning all about lemon sharks and getting the chance to swim with them. it is a fantastic experience to get up close to a shark. Lemon sharks are not aggressive which is why all ages are welcome.

Buggy Adventure

Buggy Adventure is a great way to see the sites around Cape Verde Sal. See the real heart of Cape Verde by taking trips to the many sand dunes and mountains around the area with the odd stop to enjoy an ice cream.
